Beef and Broccoli

Description

Craving Chinese food? Save money and have a restaurant-worthy meal at home with this top-rated beef and broccoli recipe.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Beef steak: cut into strips
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sherry
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cornstarch

Steps

  1. Whisk oyster sauce, sherry, sesame oil, soy sauce, sugar, and cornstarch together in a bowl; stir until sugar has dissolved.
  2. Place steak in a shallow bowl. Pour oyster sauce mixture over meat and stir to coat; cover and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add ginger and garlic; let sizzle for about 1 minute to flavor oil, then remove and discard.
  4. Stir in broccoli. Toss and stir until bright green and almost tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Remove broccoli from the wok and set aside.
  5. Add a little more oil to the wok, if needed, then add beef with marinade.
  6. Stir and toss until sauce thickens and turns shiny and meat is no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  7. Return broccoli to the wok; stir until heated through, about 3 minutes.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
